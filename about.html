About Us

Divine Design Decorations is a company that has been serving New England and surrounds for more than 10 years. We are a premier wedding and event planning company serving the North East area. We special- ize and provide high quality event planning to fit any budget, ensuring an organized, flawless, and stress-free event. We will be sure to meet the demands of our client’s needs and desires, with passion and efficiency.

Albertina Cardoso

Albertina Cardoso is the owner of Divine Design Decorations and has been an event planner since 2002. She has done inumerous parties from weddings to Sweets Sixteen and corporate events. Her natural creativity is a gift as well as her fes- tive personality, thus, allowing you to enjoy the process. With all the re- sources she has acquired through years of experience, Albertina will analyze your budget, plan your event and make it the most memorable one.
